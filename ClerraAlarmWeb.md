# ClerraAlarm Landing Page: Design & Product Vision

This document outlines the core product essence of ClerraAlarm and the design strategy for its web landing page.

## 1. Product Vision: "Conquer Your Morning"
ClerraAlarm isn't just an alarm clock; it's a **Morning Discipline Engine**. Most people fail their day in the first 10 minutes because they hit snooze or reach for their phones. Clerra ensures you are physically and mentally active before the alarm stops.

### Core Value Propositions
- **Forced Activity**: You cannot stop the alarm without completing a "Mission" (Math, Push-ups, Scanning a Photo, etc.).
- **Cinematic Atmosphere**: Waking up shouldn't be stressful—it should be intentional. Cinematic backgrounds and natural tones set the stage for a productive day.
- **Progress Tracking**: Streaks and statistics that gamify the building of discipline.

---

## 2. Design System Tokens
To maintain consistency with the mobile application, the landing page must adhere to these core values.

### Color Palette (Nature & Discipline)
| Role | Hex Code | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Accent (The Dawn)** | `#FF7F62` | Vibrant orange, used for primary actions and key highlights. |
| **Background (Parchment)** | `#FEF4EC` | Soft, warm background that feels premium and calming. |
| **Text (Deep Soil)** | `#2E1E1A` | High contrast for readability, avoids the harshness of pitch black. |
| **Subtext (Fog)** | `#A09088` | Muted tone for descriptions and secondary info. |
| **Surface (Highlight)** | `#FFE5DD` | Used for hover states and card backgrounds. |

### Typography
- **Headlines**: *Outfit* or *Inter* (Bold/ExtraBold). Clean, modern, and authoritative.
- **Body**: *Inter* or *Roboto*. Highly legible with generous line-height (1.6) for a premium editorial feel.

---

## 3. Landing Page Architecture

### Section 1: The Hero (Peak Intent)
- **Background**: A high-definition, auto-looping cinematic video of nature (e.g., fog rolling over mountains or dawn breaking over a forest).
- **Headline**: "Never Snooze Your Life Away Again."
- **Subheadline**: "ClerraAlarm transforms your first 10 minutes from a struggle into a mission. Build discipline, one morning at a time."
- **Primary CTA**: "Download for iOS & Android" (Accent Orange).

### Section 2: Choose Your Mission (The Challenge Showcase)
An interactive grid or carousel showing the "Mission Types":
- **The Brain-Hack**: Math problems to fire up the frontal lobe.
- **The Physical**: Push-ups or Jumps to get the blood flowing.
- **The Scavenger**: Photo challenges that require you to leave your bed.

### Section 3: Aesthetics of Discipline
Split-screen layout:
- **Left**: "Wake up to beauty, not noise."
- **Right**: A showcase of the **Cinematic Vibes** wallpapers (Video backgrounds) vs. **Natural Colors**.

### Section 4: Pricing / Pro
A simple, high-fidelity card titled **"Go PRO"**:
- 3-day Free Trial.
- Premium features: Unlimited Missions, All Cinematic Wallpapers, Custom Voice-overs, and Performance Analytics.

### Section 5: The Footer
Minimalist. Social links, Help Center, and "Made for those who want more out of their day."

---

## 4. Interaction Philosophy
- **Micro-Animations**: Buttons should have a subtle "lift" on hover.
- **Parallax**: Background videos should move slightly on scroll to create depth.
- **Glassmorphism**: UI cards should use high-blur backgrounds to feel like a premium OS overlay.
